Best Games To Play

Roblox

Created by David Baszucki & Erik Cassel (2006)

Roblox is one of the largest online gaming platforms in the world, with over 80 million daily active users. It allows players to create and experience millions of user-generated games across every possible genre.

Using Roblox Studio and Lua scripting, anyone can build their own games — from immersive roleplay worlds and horror experiences to simulators, tycoons, racing games, and obbies.

Fun Fact: Hits like Brookhaven, Adopt Me!, Blox Fruits, and Doors all began as small passion projects by regular users.

  • Released: September 1, 2006
  • Genre: Massive multiplayer creation platform
  • Age Rating: 5+
  • Platforms: PC, Mobile, Xbox, PlayStation
  • Best For: Creativity, socializing, and learning game design

Garry's Mod

Created by Garry Newman (Facepunch Studios, 2006)

Garry's Mod is the ultimate physics sandbox. Players get complete creative freedom to spawn objects, build crazy contraptions, and experiment with realistic physics.

It's hugely popular for roleplay servers, building contests, and chaotic multiplayer fun with thousands of addons.

Fun Fact: Over 20 million copies sold and still has one of the most active modding communities after nearly 20 years.

  • Released: November 29, 2006
  • Genre: Physics Sandbox
  • Age Rating: 12+
  • Platforms: PC (Steam), Limited console versions
  • Best For: Building, roleplay, and hilarious experiments

Hello Neighbor

Developed by Dynamic Pixels & published by tinyBuild (2017)

Hello Neighbor is a clever stealth horror game where you sneak into your neighbor’s house to discover the dark secret hidden in his basement.

The standout feature is the advanced AI that learns from your playstyle and adapts. The game blends puzzle-solving, exploration, and jump-scare horror.

Fun Fact: Before the official release, a public prototype test was held in 2016.

  • Released: December 8, 2017
  • Genre: Stealth Horror / Puzzle
  • Age Rating: 9+
  • Platforms: PC, Consoles, Mobile
  • Best For: Puzzle solving, stealth, and horror exploration
